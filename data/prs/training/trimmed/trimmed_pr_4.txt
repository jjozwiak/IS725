CLEVELAND, OH The Cleveland Indians today announced the signing of free agent of Domingo Santana to a Major League contract for the 2020 season with a club option for 2021. Santana, 27, spent the 2019 season with the Seattle Mariners, batting .253 (114-for-451) with 20 doubles, 1 triple, 21 home runs and 69 RBI in 121 games.
