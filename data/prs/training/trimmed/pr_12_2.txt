The Pittsburgh Pirates today signed outfielder Guillermo Heredia to a one-year contract for the 2020 season. The announcement was made by Pirates General Manager, Ben Cherington. Heredia, who will turn 29 on January 31, hit .225 (46-for-204) with 13 doubles, five home runs and 20 RBI in 89 games with Tampa Bay in 2019. He made 41 appearances in center field, 28 in right field and 14 in left field.
