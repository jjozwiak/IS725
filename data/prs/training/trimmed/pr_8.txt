
FORT MYERS, FL The Boston Red Sox today signed right-handed pitcher Collin McHugh to a one-year contract for the 2020 season. To make room for McHugh on the 40-man roster, the club designated right-handed pitcher Hector Velazquez for assignment. Red Sox Chief Baseball Officer Chaim Bloom made the announcement. McHugh, 32, is 58-43 with a 3.95 ERA (351 ER/800.2 IP) in 210 career major league outings, including 119 starts and 91 relief appearances.
