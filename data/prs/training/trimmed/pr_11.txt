BOSTON, MA The Boston Red Sox today claimed right-handed pitcher Phillips Valdez off waivers from the Seattle Mariners. To make room on the 40-man roster, the Red Sox placed second baseman Dustin Pedroia on the 60-day injured list. Chief Baseball Officer Chaim Bloom made the announcement. Valdez, 28, saw the first action of his major league career with the Texas Rangers last season and posted a 3.94 ERA (7 ER/16.0 IP) with nine walks and 18 strikeouts over 11 appearances, all in relief.
