DETROIT The Detroit Tigers have agreed to terms with catcher Austin Romine on a one-year Major League contract. Romine, 31, played in 73 games with the New York Yankees in 2019, hitting .281/.310/.439 with 12 doubles, eight home runs and 35 RBI in 73 games. He established career highs in batting average, on-base percentage, slugging percentage and hits (64), while tying his career high in doubles. Over his final 39 games of the season, he hit .333/.370/.548 with nine doubles, six home runs and 19 RBI.
