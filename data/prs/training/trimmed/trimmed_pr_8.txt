GLENDALE, Ariz. The Chicago White Sox have agreed to terms on a one-year, $3.5-million contract with infielder Leury Garcia, which includes a club option for 2021. Under terms of the agreement, Garcia will receive $3.25 million in 2020, while the White Sox hold a $3.5-million option for 2021 with a $250,000 buyout. Garcia, 29, previously had agreed to terms on a one-year, $3.25-million contract on January 10 to avoid arbitration.
