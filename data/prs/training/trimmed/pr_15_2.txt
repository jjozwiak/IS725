SAN DIEGO The San Diego Padres have signed right-handed pitcher Pierce Johnson to a two-year contract through the 2021 season with a club option for 2022, Executive Vice President/General Manager A.J. Preller announced today. Johnson, 28, spent the 2019 season with the Hanshin Tigers of Nippon Professional Baseball in Japan, serving as their primary setup man and posting the second-most holds (40) in the Central League. The right-hander went 2-3 with a 1.38 ERA (9 ER, 58.2 IP), 91 strikeouts against 13 walks and allowed just two home runs across his 58 appearances.
