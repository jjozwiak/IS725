DETROIT The Detroit Tigers today selected righthanded pitcher Rony Garcia from the New York Yankees with the No. 1 pick of the Major League phase of the Rule 5 Draft. Garcia, 21, split the 2019 season between Single A Tampa and Double A Trenton, posting a 4.01 ERA (130.1IP/58ER) and 129 strikeouts in 25 appearances, including 24 starts. The 6-3, 200-pound, righty posted a 1.23 WHIP and limited opponents to a .234 batting average. His 104 strikeouts and 105.1 innings pitched with Trenton were both team-best marks, while his 20 starts were tied for the club lead.
