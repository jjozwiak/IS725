CHICAGO The Chicago Cubs today acquired right-handed pitcher Travis Lakins from the Boston Red Sox for a player to be named or cash consideration. Chicago's 40-man roster now stands at 40 players. Lakins, 25, made his major league debut with Boston last season, pitching to a 3.86 ERA (10 ER/23.1 IP) in 16 games, including three starts as an opener, while walking 10 and striking out 18.
