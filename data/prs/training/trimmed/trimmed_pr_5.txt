GLENDALE, Ariz. The Chicago White Sox have agreed to terms on a five-year, $70-million contract with third baseman Yoan Moncada, plus one club option that could extend the deal through the 2025 season. Under terms of the contract, Moncada will receive a $4-million signing bonus, $1 million in 2020, $6 million in 2021, $13 million in 2022, $17 million in 2023 and $24 million in 2024. The White Sox hold an option for $25 million in 2025, with a $5-million buyout.
