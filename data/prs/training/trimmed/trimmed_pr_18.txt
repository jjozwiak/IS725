CHICAGO The Chicago Cubs and right-handed pitcher Jeremy Jeffress have agreed to terms on a 2020 contract. Terms of the deal were not disclosed. Chicago's 40-man roster now stands at 40 players. Jeffress, 32, is a career 28-11 with 44 saves, 74 holds and a 3.16 ERA (141 ER/401.0 IP) in 392 major league appearances, all but one in relief, covering all or part of 10 major league seasons with Milwaukee (2010, 2014-16, 2017-19), Kansas City (2011-12), Toronto (2013-14) and Texas (2016-17).
