SEATTLE, Wash. Seattle Mariners Executive Vice President & General Manager of Baseball Operations Jerry Dipoto announced today that the Mariners have signed free agent infielder Patrick Wisdom to a one-year contract. Per club policy, the terms of the contract were not disclosed. Seattle's 40-man roster is now at 39 players. Wisdom, 28, spent the majority of last season with Triple-A Nashville in the Rangers organization, where he hit 31 home runs and 74 RBI in 107 games with a .240 (95x396) batting average.
