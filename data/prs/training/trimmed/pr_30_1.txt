ST. LOUIS, MO., January 22, 2020 The St. Louis Cardinals announced today that they have agreed to terms with free-agent catcher Matt Wieters (pronounced WEE-ters) on a one-year contract for the upcoming 2020 season. Wieters, 33, returns to the Cardinals for a second-straight season. He signed with St. Louis on February 27 last year as a non-roster invitee, and was a key member of the Redbirds' N.L. Central Division-winning club, appearing in 67 games (54 starts).
