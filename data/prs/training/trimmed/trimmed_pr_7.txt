CLEVELAND, OH The Cleveland Indians today announced the signing of free agent Cameron Rupp to a Minor League contract with an invitation to Major League Spring Training camp. Rupp, 31, is a veteran of five Major League seasons (2013-17) with the Philadelphia Phillies, posting a career average of .234 (240-for-1027) with 57 2B, 39 HR and 124 RBI in 296 games.
