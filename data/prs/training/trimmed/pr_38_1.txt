DETROIT The Detroit Tigers today acquired righthanded pitcher Dario Agrazal from the Pittsburgh Pirates in exchange for cash considerations. Agrazal, 24, saw time at three levels of the Pirates organization in 2019, including his first Major League action. In 15 appearances, including 14 starts, with Pittsburgh, Agrazal had a 4-5 record with a 4.91 ERA (73.1IP/40ER) and 41 strikeouts.
